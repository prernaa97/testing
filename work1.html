Social media refers to online platforms where people connect, share, and interact with content in real-time. It has changed how we communicate, do business, and consume information.
Social media is a powerful digital platform that allows people to connect, share, and interact globally. It has transformed communication, business, and entertainment through apps like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok. While it offers benefits like instant news, marketing opportunities, and creative expression, it also comes with challenges such as privacy concerns, misinformation, and addiction. With trends like AI-driven content, short videos, and influencer marketing, social media continues to evolve, shaping how we engage with the world.
